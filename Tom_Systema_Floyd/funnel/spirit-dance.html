Program Details

A Studio Built on Pride & Progress.

Spirit Dance runs alongside our martial arts programs, same values, different movement vocabulary. Kids build technique through pom, hip-hop, and expressive dance styles, all led by a certified instructor with years of performance experience.

We focus on progress over perfection. Every kid shows growth, and every family gets to see it at our end-of-session showcase.

  • Ages 5–14, age-appropriate grouping with a mix of beginner and intermediate levels
  • Weekly sessions, consistent, habit-building practice schedule
  • Showcase performances, multiple chances to perform for family & friends
  • Certified instruction, led by a vetted, trained dance coach
  • Welcoming environment, come see the vibe before committing
