About The Master

A Rare Visit From The Source.

Vladimir Vasiliev is the world's foremost teacher of Systema, the Russian martial art rooted in breath, structure, and effortless movement. Through Systema HQ in Toronto, he has trained instructors and students across more than 40 countries.

This October, that lineage comes to Florida. You'll get hands-on time with Vladimir, plus instruction from Systema teachers traveling in from across North America, all under one roof for two full days.

40+ Years Experience Taught In 40+ Countries Founder, Systema HQ Toronto Author Of Many Works On Systema
What You'll Study

Five Core Principles.

Tactical Relaxation is a Systema fundamental: the ability to stay tension-free under pressure. The festival dives deep into the five pillars that build elite-level resilience for both combat and daily life.

01

Breathing

Reset your nervous system on demand, so you can stay tension-free through impact, fatigue, and confrontation.

02

Structure

Stand and move from your bones, so you can hold your ground without locking up, even when you're struck.

03

Smooth Movement

Eliminate hitches and pauses, so you can disappear and reappear before the opponent reads your intent.

04

Short Work

Close-range striking, deflection, and control, so you can resolve a confrontation in inches when there's no time to wind up.

05

Internal Work

Train the mental and emotional layer, so you can stay composed and decisive when fear normally takes over.
