FAQ

Parent Questions.

What ages do you accept?

Systema Floyd welcomes students from age 2.5 through adults. Our after-school program at partner campuses focuses on kids roughly ages 5–14, with age-appropriate groupings. For toddler classes (ages 2.5–5) and teen/adult classes, we run sessions at our West Palm Beach studio, just ask on the form.

Do you pick up from the classroom?

Yes, our instructors coordinate directly with campus staff. Your child is escorted from their classroom or the designated pickup area to our class space.

How much does it cost?

Pricing varies slightly by campus and session length. The easiest way to get exact numbers is to request a trial, we'll send pricing for your specific campus along with the trial confirmation.

What does my child need to bring?

For the first class, just comfortable athletic clothes and a water bottle. Once enrolled, you'll receive a uniform (gi) and a belt. We provide everything else.

Can my child try a class first?

Absolutely. The first class is always free, no pressure, no commitment. Book a trial here.

Do you run during summer and school breaks?

Our After School program runs with the academic calendar. During summer and extended breaks, we offer our full-day camps.

Ready to get your child started?

Pick your campus. Pick a day. We'll handle the rest.
