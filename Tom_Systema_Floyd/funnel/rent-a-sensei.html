How Rent a Sensei Works

Active Babysitting, Done Right

What Rent a Sensei is, and what it isn't, before you book.

This Is

In-Home, Active Babysitting

  • Energetic, professional Senseis trained on Systema standards.
  • Structured games, age-appropriate drills, movement breaks.
  • Three-hour minimum bookings, in your home.
  • Background-checked staff, clear policies, simple checkout.
This Is Not

A Party or Event Service

  • Not for birthday parties or large group bookings.
  • Not a one-off entertainer or character performer.
  • Not a martial arts class replacement (book After School for that).

Common Questions

Before You Book

The ones we hear most. If yours isn't here, just ask.

How do I book a Sensei?

Reach out through our contact page with the date, the hours you need, your kids' ages, and your home address. We'll confirm availability, quote any travel fee, and lock in your Sensei.

How does the travel fee work?

We base travel fees on distance and driving time from our West Palm Beach studio to your home. You'll see the exact fee, if any, before you confirm and pay.

What does my Sensei bring?

Energy, structure, and a plan. Most equipment your kids need for active play is already in your home, but your Sensei may bring small training tools where appropriate.

What ages do you take?

Six months and up. For ages 6 months to 3 years, your booking will be staffed by a female Sensei. For ages 4 and up, your Sensei may be male or female based on availability.

What's your cancellation policy?

Plans change. We ask for as much notice as possible so we can free up your Sensei's schedule.

Are Senseis background-checked?

Yes. Every Sensei in our Rent a Sensei program is background-checked and trained on Systema Floyd standards before they're matched with families.

What's your service area?

We're based in West Palm Beach and serve the surrounding communities. Travel fees apply based on distance from our studio.
