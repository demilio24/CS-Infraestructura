Common Questions

FAQ

Quick answers to the things parents ask most. Need something specific? Reach out and we will walk you through it.

How do I book a party?

Reach out through our contact form and tell us your preferred date, location, and theme. Our team will follow up with availability and a quote, and walk you through the deposit and final details.

How early should I book?

We recommend booking 4 to 6 weeks in advance, especially for weekend slots. Holiday weekends and peak birthday months book out faster, so the earlier the better.

Can I bring my own decor and cake?

Yes. You are welcome to bring your own decorations, cake, and food. If you would rather we handle setup or work with our balloon vendor, just let us know in advance and we will coordinate.

Do you provide food?

Food is not included by default. Most families bring pizza, snacks, and drinks. We can recommend local vendors or coordinate with your caterer if needed.

What ages are these parties for?

We host birthdays for kids of all ages. Activities, intensity, and pacing are tailored to the birthday child's age group, so a 5-year-old's party looks very different from a 12-year-old's.

What happens if it rains for an outdoor setup?

For outdoor parties, we monitor the forecast in the days leading up to the event and coordinate with you on a backup plan, whether that is a covered area, our gym, or a rescheduled date. Outdoor balloon installs include a weather-risk fee for added reinforcement.

What is the cancellation policy?

Cancellation terms are explained when you book. In general, deposits secure your date and are partially or fully refundable depending on how far in advance you cancel.

Ready to Lock In the Date?

Tell us your theme, your date, and your venue. We will send back a quote and walk you through every detail. Most parties book in under a week.
