A Day at Camp

Packed. Purposeful. Fun.

Every hour earns its keep. No screens, no filler, no bored kids.

8:00 – 9:00 AM

Arrival & Warm-Up

Drop-off, group stretch, and a morning character huddle to set the day's focus.

9:00 – 10:30 AM

Martial Arts Block

Drills, technique, partner work. Ranked by age + skill group.

10:30 AM – 12:00 PM

Outdoor Challenge

Obstacle courses, team games, and movement play outside.

12:00 – 1:00 PM

Lunch & Reset

Bring-your-own lunch, quiet table time, and a guided cool-down.

1:00 – 2:30 PM

Skill Workshop

Rotating workshops: weapon work (age-safe), grappling basics, mental focus drills.

2:30 – 4:00 PM

Water / Field Play

Splash games, capture-the-flag, or group challenges — weather dependent.

4:00 – 5:00 PM

Belt Recap & Pickup

Reflection, a belt-recap demo, and parent pickup with that day's highlights.
