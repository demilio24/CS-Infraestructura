### FAQ items (single version)

**Q1. What lesson formats do you offer?**
Private 1:1 (one instructor, one swimmer), semi-private (1:2 or 1:3 for siblings or friends), and adaptive lessons for neurodiverse swimmers. All formats focus on water safety first.

**Q2. What ages do you teach?**
Six months to adult. We adjust pace, format, and progression to fit every age group.

**Q3. Where are your lessons located?**
Six fixed locations across Vancouver Island plus mobile-to-your-pool service from Victoria to Campbell River. See the Locations section above for the full list.

**Q4. How do I book?**
Online at aquanautsacademy.janeapp.com, by email at info@aquanautsacademy.ca, or call or text 250-327-3212.

**Q5. How often should my child take lessons?**
Once a week is the minimum we recommend for steady progress. Twice or three times a week is available for accelerated learning when scheduling allows.

**Q6. How long is each lesson?**
30 minutes for most swimmers under 5. 60 minutes for older kids, adults, and anyone who wants more time for skill work.

**Q7. My child is afraid of the water. Can you still help?**
Yes. Fear of water is one of the most common reasons families come to us. Our instructors use gentle, patient techniques and never force a step before your child is ready.

**Q8. Can siblings or friends take lessons together?**
Yes. Family lessons (siblings) and semi-private lessons (two families together) are available at lower per-swimmer rates.

**Q9. What is your cancellation policy?**
One free make-up lesson per month with 24-hour notice. Cancellations under 2 hours may carry a small fee so we can pay our instructor for the booked time.
