# Maria / Safe Space Swim: private swim lessons across San Diego County

## What this is
Coach Maria runs Safe Space Swim, private one-on-one swimming lessons for children and adults across San Diego County. Single-page sales site covering the full progression method (water safety through swim-team ready), credentials (11+ years experience, CPR & Water Safety certified, 5-star rated).

## Architecture
- `safespaceswim.html` : single marketing / sales page
- Tech: HTML + embedded CSS/JS, GitHub Pages, GHL iframe embed (standard pattern across all client funnels in this repo)
