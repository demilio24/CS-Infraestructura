## CONTENT STRATEGY OVERVIEW

Every piece of content falls into one of three pillars. The mix is intentional. Mechanism content builds trust by showing you actually know what you're doing. Proof content removes doubt. Identity content makes people feel like they know you personally. Combined, they move a stranger from "who is this guy" to "I need to talk to him" in about 30 days.

**Pillar 1: Mechanism (40%)** -- Show what you do and how it works. Teach the system. Pull back the curtain on the actual strategies, frameworks, and tools. This is the anti-agency play. Most agencies hide behind vague promises. We show the machine.

**Pillar 2: Proof (35%)** -- Client results, case studies, specific numbers. Not "we got great results." Actual screenshots, actual ROAS, actual conversion rates. Numbers do the selling.

**Pillar 3: Identity (25%)** -- Who you are, why you're different, behind the scenes. The stuff that makes people feel connected to Emilio as a person and a business owner, not just a service provider. Hot takes, origin stories, day-in-the-life, honest opinions.

### SCRIPT 5: "Why Google Ads Beat Social Media Ads"

**Pillar:** Mechanism
**[Estimated length: 50-60 seconds]**

**HOOK:**
If you run a local service business and you're spending money on Facebook or Instagram ads, I need you to hear this.

**BODY:**
There's a massive difference between interruption marketing and intent marketing.

Facebook and Instagram are interruption. Someone's scrolling, looking at memes, watching reels. Your ad pops up for a roofing company. They don't need a roofer right now. Maybe they'll remember you later. Probably not.

Google is intent. Someone types "emergency plumber near me" at 11pm. They have a broken pipe. They need you right now. That is the highest quality lead you will ever get.

And look, this is why our clients see an 11x return on ad spend. We're not trying to convince people they need a service. We're showing up the exact second they're already searching for it.

So for local service businesses, Google Ads just wins. Every single time. It's not even close.

The leads are better. They close faster. And you're not competing with cat videos for attention.

**CTA:**
Comment "GOOGLE" if you want to see how this works for your industry.
