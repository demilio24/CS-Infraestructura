LowCountry Swim School Enrollment & Billing Agreement

This Billing & Enrollment Agreement (“Agreement”) governs the enrollment of students in LowCountry Swim School (“LCSS”) programs. By enrolling, the undersigned parent or guardian (“Registrant”) acknowledges, understands, and agrees to be bound by the terms set forth herein.

1. Program Structure and Commitment

1.1. LCSS operates on a subscription-based enrollment model.
1.2. A minimum two-month (8-week) commitment is required prior to any pause or withdrawal.
1.3. Enrollment and billing continue automatically unless properly paused or withdrawn in accordance with this Agreement.
1.4. Missed lessons, absences, illness, vacations, or schedule conflicts do not suspend enrollment or billing.

2. Withdrawal and Cancellation Policy

2.1. A minimum thirty (30) days’ written notice is required to withdraw from the program.
2.2. Withdrawal is valid only upon submission of the official LCSS online withdrawal form.
2.3. The withdrawal form must be submitted on or before the 1st day of the month to terminate enrollment for the following month.
2.4. Withdrawal requests submitted after the 5th day of the month will not be accepted, and tuition for the following month remains due in full.
2.5. A $75 late withdrawal fee will be assessed if proper notice is not given.
2.6. Verbal notice, email, phone calls, text messages, or in-person communication are not valid forms of withdrawal.

3. Tuition and Billing

3.1. Tuition is billed monthly on the last Monday of each month for the upcoming month.
3.2. Tuition rates vary based on lesson frequency and program type.
3.3. Tuition must be paid in full to secure and maintain a lesson schedule.
3.4. Failure to attend scheduled lessons does not relieve financial responsibility.

4. Refund Policy

4.1. Before lessons begin and are scheduled: Fifty percent (50%) of tuition may be refunded; the registration fee is non-refundable.
4.2. Before lessons begin and are not scheduled: Only the $50 registration fee is non-refundable.
4.3. Once lessons have commenced, no refunds are issued for any reason, including illness, injury, relocation, dissatisfaction, or schedule changes.
4.4. Credits, if issued, are not refunds and carry no cash value.

5. Credit Policy

5.1. Credits are issued solely at LCSS’s discretion and only under circumstances expressly approved by management.
5.2. Credits are non-transferable, non-refundable, and not redeemable for cash, registration fees, or prior balances.
5.3. Credits expire one (1) year from the date of issuance and are automatically forfeited if unused.
5.4. Credits may be applied only while the student remains actively enrolled and financially committed to the program.
5.5. Credits may not be used to hold or reserve a schedule without ongoing enrollment and tuition payments.
5.6. Withdrawal from the program results in forfeiture of any unused credits.

6. Payment Method Requirements

6.1. A valid credit card must remain on file at all times.
6.2. LCSS does not accept cash, checks, Venmo, Zelle, or alternative payment methods.

7. Declined or Failed Payments

7.1. A $25 processing fee will be charged for each declined or failed transaction.
7.2. After three (3) declined transactions, LCSS reserves the right to revoke automatic billing privileges and terminate enrollment.
7.3. Students may not participate in lessons until all outstanding balances and fees are paid in full.

8. Discounts

8.1. Discounts are applied at the time of billing and cannot be combined.
8.2. 10% disount- “A 10% discount is available for siblings, active military, or seniors (65+), applied to the lowest-priced lesson for one student per family only. Discounts cannot be combined or stacked with any other offers.”
8.3. 15% discount applies to family enrollment (one adult and one child; lowest-priced lesson).
8.4. Families enrolled in more than two lessons per week may receive an additional discount.
8.5. If another discount is already applied, an additional 5% discount applies to additional lessons only.
8.6. Discounts may be modified or discontinued at LCSS’s discretion with prior notice.

9. Scholarships

9.1. Scholarship balances must be paid in full prior to lesson participation.
9.2. Families are responsible for renewing scholarships or assuming tuition payments to maintain enrollment.
9.3. Scholarships do not supersede billing, withdrawal, credit, or attendance policies.

10. Authorization for Automatic Charges

10.1. By enrolling, I authorize LCSS to automatically charge the payment method on file for all tuition, fees, and applicable charges.
10.2. LCSS will provide notice of changes to tuition or fees no fewer than five (5) days in advance.

11. Enforcement and Legal Acknowledgment

11.1. Failure to comply with this Agreement may result in continued billing, forfeiture of credits, suspension of lessons, or termination of enrollment, at LCSS’s sole discretion.
11.2. I acknowledge that I have read, understand, and voluntarily agree to be bound by all terms contained in this Agreement.

12. Governing Law (Optional but Recommended)

This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of South Carolina, without regard to its conflict-of-law provisions.

Renewal & Withdrawal Policy


Automatic Renewal & Commitment

LowCountry Swim School (LCSS) operates on a subscription-based enrollment.

  • Enrollment automatically renews each month.

  • Families commit to a minimum of two months (8 weeks) upon enrollment.

  • Tuition is automatically charged on the last Monday of each month.

  • Enrollment will continue until a proper withdrawal or pause notice is submitted and approved.

Withdrawal & Pause Notice Requirements

A 30-day written notice is required to withdraw or pause lessons.

  • Deadline: The official withdrawal/pause form must be received by the 1st of the month to cancel for the following month.

  • No withdrawals or pauses are accepted after the 1st.

  • Forms submitted after the 5th of the month will not be accepted, and you will remain responsible for the full month’s tuition.

  • We do not accept email, phone calls, text messages, or in-person requests.

How to Submit

All withdrawals or pauses must be submitted using the official form available on our website:
www.lowcountryswimschool.com

Late Withdrawal Fee

If you are unable to commit to the next month and submit a withdrawal after the deadline, an automatic $75 late withdrawal fee will be charged to your account at the time the withdrawal notice is received.

After Withdrawal Is Processed

  • No further tuition will be charged after the notice period is fulfilled.

  • You may complete any remaining lessons already paid for.

  • Missed lessons must be made up during your final month of enrollment.

  • Unused make-up lessons do not carry over.

Important Notes

  • Withdrawals for medical reasons are considered on a case-by-case basis.

  • Tuition cannot be prorated due to our billing cycle.

  • Failure to submit proper notice means enrollment and billing will continue.


  • For questions or assistance, visit www.lowcountryswimschool.com.

  • A non-refundable $50 registration fee is due at enrollment: $45 registration, $5 donated to the Hope Floats Foundation.

  • Lessons operate on a subscription-based model and automatically renew monthly.

  • Tuition is charged monthly and continues unless proper withdrawal notice is submitted.

2. Withdrawal & Cancellation

  • A 30-day written notice is required to withdraw from the program.

  • Withdrawal forms submitted after the 5th of the month will not be accepted, and tuition for the following month remains due in full.

  • Email, text, phone, or in-person notices are not valid forms of withdrawal.

  • Late withdrawals incur an automatic $75 fee if the following month is not committed.

3. Lesson Cancellations & Make-Ups

  • 24-hour notice is required to reschedule a lesson.

  • Each student is limited to two reschedules per month.

  • Make-ups are subject to availability, must be completed within the same month, and are not guaranteed with the same instructor.

  • No-call/no-show lessons are forfeited.

4. Weather, Pool & Instructor Changes

  • LCSS provides no refunds for weather-related or facility closures; make-ups or credits may be offered at LCSS’s discretion.

  • Instructor substitutions or schedule adjustments may occur due to emergencies, illness, or operational needs.

5. Credit Policy

  • Credits are issued solely at the discretion of LCSS and only for circumstances expressly approved by management.

  • Credits are non-transferable, non-refundable, and carry no cash value.

  • Credits expire one (1) year from the date of issuance and are automatically forfeited if unused.

  • Credits may only be applied while the student remains actively enrolled and financially committed to the program.

  • Credits may not be used to hold or reserve a schedule without ongoing enrollment and tuition payments.

  • Withdrawal from the program results in forfeiture of any unused credits.

6. Additional Terms

  • Lessons do not carry over month to month.

  • Tuition cannot be prorated.

  • Medical withdrawals are reviewed on a case-by-case basis.

By enrolling, I acknowledge that I have read, understand, and agree to all policies herein, and that failure to comply may result in continued billing, forfeiture of credits, suspension, or termination of enrollment.
