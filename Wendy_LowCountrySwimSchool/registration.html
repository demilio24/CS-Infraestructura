By enrolling with LowCountry Swim School, you agree to our billing and enrollment terms. Monthly tuition is due on the 1st of each month. A 30-day written notice is required for cancellations. No refunds are issued for missed classes.

I understand that swimming involves inherent risks. I voluntarily waive any claims against LowCountry Swim School, its owners, instructors, or staff for injury or loss arising from participation in swim lessons, except in cases of gross negligence.

Rainout & Withdrawal Policy
