Who runs this

Hi, I'm Emilio. I started Nils because every $1M+ local service owner I knew was trapped running their own business.

Six years later we install the marketing systems that bring leads in and the automations that take busywork out, so owners can build instead of maintain.

Six years, four chapters.

2019

Started as a one-man shop building websites for solo operators. The thesis: a sharp site can punch way above its price tag for local businesses.

2021

Added Google Ads management. Clients kept hitting the same ceiling: a good site doesn't matter if nobody finds it.

2023

Built the review-automation layer that turned every happy customer into a referral engine. Now a core part of the marketing stack.

2025

Launched the automation audit. Existing clients kept asking us to take the manual work out of their operations, so we systemized the answer.

What working with Nils actually looks like.

Question 01

How long does the marketing system take to install?

The site goes live in 3 to 4 weeks. Ads light up the week the site does. The review-automation layer hooks in shortly after. By month two you should already be seeing the leading indicators move: more form fills, more booked calls, more reviews landing on autopilot.

Question 02

What does the automation audit actually deliver?

A department-by-department blueprint of every manual job in your business that AI or automation can take over, scored by hours saved per month and difficulty to build. You get the document plus a working session to walk through it. You can build it yourself, hire us to install it, or sit on it. No upsell pressure.

Question 03

Do you take on businesses under $1M?

Sometimes, but rarely. The honest answer: most of what we install pays for itself fastest at the $1M to $10M stage, where there's enough volume to make the system pay back inside a few months. Below that we usually point owners to lighter-weight tools and check back in a year.

Question 04

What happens if it doesn't work?

If the marketing system doesn't double your conversion rate inside 60 days, we refund every dollar of the build fee. If the audit doesn't surface enough automation opportunity to pay for itself in year one, same deal. Boring guarantees, on purpose.
