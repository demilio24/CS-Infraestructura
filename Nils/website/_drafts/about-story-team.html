How we got here.

Nils Digital started in 2019 as a one-man shop building websites for solo operators. By 2021 we'd added Google Ads management because clients kept hitting the same ceiling: a good site doesn't matter if nobody finds it. By 2023 we'd built the review-automation layer that turned every happy customer into a referral. And in 2025 we launched the automation audit, because existing clients started asking us to take the manual work out of their operations.

What hasn't changed is the model. Operator-led. Small team. Every job goes through Emilio's hands. We've never been the cheapest agency or the biggest, but we've stayed the one local service owners recommend to each other.

The team behind the work.

Emilio, Founder & Lead Operator

I started Nils Digital in 2019 because I kept watching $1M+ local service owners get crushed by their own businesses. They were great operators, but they were spending their weeks doing the work of a marketer, a recruiter, and a bookkeeper all at once.

Six years in, the agency is small on purpose. I'm in every audit, every build, every revision. I'd rather run a team of four that ships great work than a team of forty that ships the same brief to thirty clients.

Outside of Nils I'm usually building something else: a side project, a workflow, a way to take ten clicks out of someone's day. The work doesn't really stop. I just like it that way.
