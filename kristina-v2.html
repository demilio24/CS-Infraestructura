Everything You Need to Know

We know you have questions. Here are the answers to the most common ones we hear from Auburn and Opelika families.

What age range do you teach?

+
We specialize in teaching infants, toddlers, and young children ‚Äî typically between 6 months and 6 years old. Our curriculum is designed specifically for this age group, focusing on gentle water introduction, comfort-building, and foundational swim skills.

Where do lessons take place?

+
All lessons are held in our private, heated home pool in Opelika, AL. It's a calm, safe, and distraction-free environment ‚Äî perfect for young learners. The address is shared once you book your first lesson.

How long are the lessons?

+
Each private lesson is 30 minutes long. For young children, 30 minutes is the ideal length ‚Äî long enough to make real progress, but short enough to keep them engaged and avoid fatigue.

Do parents get in the water?

+
For our youngest swimmers (typically under 2 years old), we encourage parent participation in the water. For older toddlers and young children, lessons are usually one-on-one with the instructor while parents watch from poolside. We'll work with you to decide what's best for your child.

What if my child is afraid of the water?

+
This is exactly why we do what we do. Many of the children we teach are nervous or fearful around water ‚Äî often because of a past negative experience. Our gentle, patient approach is designed to rebuild trust and confidence. We go at your child's pace, always.

How many lessons does it take to learn to swim?

+
Every child is different. Some kids gain water confidence in just a few lessons, while others need more time to build skills and comfort. On average, families book 4-8 lessons to see meaningful progress. We'll give you honest feedback after your first lesson about what to expect.

What's your cancellation policy?

+
We ask for at least 24 hours' notice if you need to cancel or reschedule. We understand life happens ‚Äî especially with young kids ‚Äî and we'll always work with you to find a new time that fits your schedule.

Are you CPR and First Aid certified?

+
Absolutely. Our instructors are fully certified in CPR, First Aid, and swim instruction. Your child's safety is our top priority, and we maintain all required certifications to ensure you have complete peace of mind.
