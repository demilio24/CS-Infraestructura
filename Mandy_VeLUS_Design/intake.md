## Services & Packages
We offer full-service residential interior design focused on renovations and reimagined homes. Our work includes space planning, interior architecture direction, material and finish selection, custom furnishings, and full project coordination from concept through completion.

Each project is completely bespoke and developed through a highly collaborative process. We do not offer predefined packages, as every home requires a tailored approach based on its architecture, scope, and client vision. Projects typically begin with an in-depth consultation and site review, followed by a detailed design phase and full execution oversight.

## Process Steps

**01. Discovery Call**
We begin by understanding your project scope, lifestyle needs, and overall vision for the space. This initial conversation allows us to determine alignment and identify the potential of the project.

**02. In-Home Consultation**
We visit the space to evaluate existing conditions, understand architectural context, and begin shaping early design direction through observation and initial recommendations.

**03. Design Presentation**
We present a fully developed design concept, including space planning, material direction, finishes, and furnishings. This phase is collaborative, allowing for refinement and alignment before moving forward.

**04. Procurement + Coordination**
We manage the sourcing and procurement of all specified furnishings, fixtures, and materials, coordinating with vendors and tracking timelines to ensure seamless execution.

**05. Installation + Completion**
We oversee the full installation process, bringing the design to life through placement, detailing, and final styling to ensure a cohesive and fully realized interior.

## FAQ

**Q: What types of projects does VeLUS Interiors take on?**
A: We specialize in full-scope residential renovations and furnishings, including kitchens, bathrooms, custom millwork, and complete home transformations. Each project is supported by comprehensive design and project management to ensure a cohesive, fully realized result.

**Q: Do you offer design-only services?**
A: Our primary focus is on full-service design paired with project management to maintain alignment from concept through completion. Select design-only engagements may be considered depending on scope and project needs.

**Q: What is your design process?**
A: Our process is structured into five phases: Discovery Call, In-Home Consultation, Design Presentation, Procurement and Coordination, and Installation and Completion. Design and project management are integrated throughout, ensuring clarity, accountability, and a seamless progression from vision to execution.

**Q: How are your fees structured?**
A: Design fees are established based on the scope and complexity of the project. Following design approval, furnishings, materials, and labor are managed and coordinated as part of the overall project. We provide transparency across both design and project management from the outset.

**Q: Do you work with my contractor or provide one?**
A: We can collaborate with your preferred contractor or introduce trusted trade partners. In either case, we provide ongoing project management to oversee execution, maintain alignment with the design, and ensure all details are carried through properly.

**Q: How involved do I need to be in the process?**
A: We guide both the design and project management, allowing you to be as involved as you prefer. Our role is to streamline decisions, coordinate all moving parts, and manage the details so your experience remains focused and efficient.

**Q: What is the typical timeline for a project?**
A: Timelines vary depending on scope, level of customization, and material availability. Through active project management, we establish clear expectations early and maintain oversight to keep the project moving efficiently.

**Q: Can you work within my budget?**
A: We design with intention and provide guidance on where to invest for the greatest impact. With project management in place, we also help monitor spending and align selections with your overall budget.

**Q: Do you handle purchasing and deliveries?**
A: Yes. We manage sourcing, procurement, vendor coordination, and delivery logistics. This is a key part of our project management approach, ensuring everything arrives and is installed as intended.

**Q: What makes VeLUS Interiors different from other design firms?**
A: Our work is defined by a refined aesthetic, disciplined process, and integrated project management. Every detail is curated and executed with intention, resulting in spaces that feel cohesive, elevated, and never arbitrary or excessive.

**Q: When should I bring in a designer?**
A: The earlier, the better. Involving us at the beginning allows both design and project management to guide the process from the start, preventing costly revisions and ensuring a more cohesive outcome.

**Q: How do I get started?**
A: We begin with a Discovery Call to understand your project and determine alignment. From there, we guide you through both the design and project management process with clarity and intention.
