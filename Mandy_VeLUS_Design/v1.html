Mandy Velus

Founder, VELUS Interiors

Mandy is the founder and principal designer of VELUS INTERIORS, a Chicago-based studio focused on creating interiors defined by restraint, material clarity, and intention.

Her work is guided by an instinct for composition, where form, texture, and spatial rhythm are considered in quiet relationship to one another. Each interior is approached as a complete environment, shaped to feel both composed and lived in.

Rather than following trends, she begins with curiosity. Each project unfolds as a dialogue, resulting in spaces that feel personal, considered, and enduring.

With a background in interior design and project management, she leads each project from concept through completion with clarity and precision, ensuring that the integrity of the design is carried through in every detail.

As both a designer and a mother, she understands that the most successful interiors are not only visually resolved, but thoughtfully lived in.

Through VELUS INTERIORS, she continues to shape residential and commercial spaces across Chicago with a distinct and disciplined point of view.
