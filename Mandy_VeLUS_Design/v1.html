Mandy Velus

Founder, VELUS Interiors

Mandy Velus is the founder and principal designer of VELUS Interiors, a luxury residential studio founded in 2018.

Raised in Guyana, a country of extraordinary natural richness, Mandy grew up watching her father, a professional photographer, find the essence of things through a lens. It was there she learned that design begins not with ideas, but with attention.

Her work is defined by a disciplined approach to composition, where space is understood through material, proportion, and light rather than style or reference. Each project is developed as a study in how environments are experienced over time. Not just how they appear in a photograph.

She does not design through repetition or aesthetic formula. Each interior is approached as its own inquiry, shaped through observation, dialogue, and restraint, resulting in work that feels composed, personal, and enduring.

With a foundation in both design and project management, she leads every project from concept through completion with exacting precision, ensuring continuity between intent, execution, and lived experience. As a designer and a mother, she understands that a home is more than what it looks like. It is how it feels to live inside it every day, a structure for daily rhythm, shaped as much by function as by feeling.

Through VELUS Interiors, she continues to shape residential interiors across Chicago and beyond, defined by clarity, restraint, and a quiet sense of permanence.

Practice

Full-Service Design & Remodel

01

New Construction

We engage from the earliest architectural stages, contributing spatial direction as structure takes form. Working in alignment with architects and builders, we shape interiors that are considered from the blueprint forward, ensuring clarity of proportion, material intent, and spatial rhythm.

02

Renovation Design

Existing spaces are approached with precision rather than sentiment. We reassess structure, flow, and material presence, refining what already exists into a more coherent spatial language. Each renovation is resolved through context, not repetition, allowing the architecture to dictate its own evolution.

03

Full Home Furnishing

We compose complete interior environments through a disciplined layering of furnishings, lighting, and material tone. Each selection is intentional within the broader spatial composition, resulting in interiors that feel resolved, continuous, and quietly intentional in their function and form.
