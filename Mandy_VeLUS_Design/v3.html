Chicago | Interior Design Studio

What We Do

Since its founding, VeLUS Interiors has been dedicated to delivering exceptional design services, ranging from expansive renovations and new constructions to single-room makeovers and everything in between. We approach every project with a fresh perspective that is tailored to meet the specific needs and desires of our clients.

Our SERVICES

Full Home Design −

We bring a thoughtful and creative eye to complete home design projects. We seamlessly integrate into the architect and contractor's teams so that we can bring our expertise and keen eye for detail into the project, from concept through installation.

Renovations +

Whether transforming a single room or reimagining an entire property, our renovation work is guided by proportion, texture, and spatial rhythm. Every decision is considered in quiet relationship to the whole, resulting in spaces that feel resolved and enduring.

Full Home Furnishings +

Curated furniture, art, and accessories selected to reflect your lifestyle with precision. Each piece is chosen for how it lives within the space — integrating seamlessly into your daily life while elevating the design with intention and care.

Meet the Founder

Mandy Velus

Mandy is the founder and principal designer of VeLUS Interiors, a Chicago-based studio focused on creating interiors defined by restraint, material clarity, and intention.

Her work is guided by an instinct for composition, where form, texture, and spatial rhythm are considered in quiet relationship to one another. Each interior is approached as a complete environment, shaped to feel both composed and lived in.

Rather than following trends, she begins with curiosity. Each project unfolds as a dialogue, resulting in spaces that feel personal, considered, and enduring.

With a background in interior design and project management, she leads each project from concept through completion with clarity and precision, ensuring that the integrity of the design is carried through in every detail.

Our PROCESS

01.
Discovery Call
Gather initial project details and understand your needs, preferences, vision for your space, and the scope of the project.
02.
Consultation
Physically visit the space, evaluate what is there, explore design options, and offer personalized suggestions to kickstart your project.
03.
Design Presentation
Present a tailored design concept, present selected furniture and design elements, and engage in discussions to refine and finalize the proposed design.
04.
Sourcing & Ordering
Coordinate the sourcing and ordering of furnishings, fixtures, and fabrics, including placing orders with suppliers, arranging for timely delivery, and tracking shipments.
05.
Installation & Reveal
The best part! Executing the design vision and bringing it to life, overseeing the entire installation process, from construction and painting to furnishing and accessorizing.
