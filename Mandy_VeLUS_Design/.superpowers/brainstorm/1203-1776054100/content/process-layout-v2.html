D. Cinematic Strips

Full-width horizontal strips — each has a photo thumbnail on the left and text on the right. On mobile, the photo goes on top as a banner. Clean, scannable, image-rich. Each strip's photo shows the actual work for that phase (measuring tape for discovery, material samples for design, etc.).
