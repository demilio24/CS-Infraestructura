Our Process — Five Phases

A disciplined sequence, from inquiry to reveal.

01
Inquiry + Fit Review
Projects begin with a considered inquiry. Scope, timeline, and alignment are reviewed to determine whether the studio is the right fit.
02
Initial Consultation
If aligned, an initial consultation follows to explore the space, intent, and direction of the project.
03
Design Development
The full interior concept is developed through spatial planning, material direction, finish selection, and composition.
04
Execution + Coordination
Implementation is overseen in close coordination with contractors and trades, ensuring design intent is carried through with precision.
05
Installation + Final Reveal
Furnishings, styling, and final detailing complete a cohesive and fully realized interior.

Principal Designer

Mandy Velus

Founder, VeLUS Interiors

Mandy Velus is the founder and principal designer of VeLUS Interiors, a Chicago-based studio founded in 2018.

Her work is defined by a disciplined approach to composition, where space is understood through material, proportion, and light rather than style or reference. Each project is developed as a study in how environments are experienced over time.

She does not design through repetition or aesthetic formula. Each interior is approached as its own inquiry, shaped through observation, dialogue, and restraint, resulting in work that feels composed, personal, and enduring.

With a background in interior design and project management, she leads each project from concept through completion with precision, ensuring continuity between intent, execution, and lived experience.

She understands interiors as more than visual composition. They are structures for daily rhythm, shaped as much by function as by feeling.

Through VeLUS Interiors, she continues to shape residential interiors across Chicago defined by clarity, restraint, and a quiet sense of permanence.

Each project reflects a considered approach to space, material, and the way it is lived in.
