Mandy Velus

Founder, VeLUS INTERIORS

Mandy Velus is the founder of VeLUS INTERIORS, a Chicago-based studio working across residential interiors defined by clarity, restraint, and spatial precision.

Her approach to design is shaped by a sensitivity to how spaces are experienced — not only in their construction, but in their atmosphere, rhythm, and quiet shifts over time.

There is a tendency to observe before defining. Each project begins with understanding how a space holds light, how it responds to movement, and how it supports the way it is lived in.

The work moves between intuition and structure — between what is sensed and what is built.

With a background in interior design and project management, she maintains continuity between vision and execution, ensuring that intention is carried through every stage of the process.

VeLUS INTERIORS continues to shape interiors that feel composed and grounded — where clarity is not imposed but gradually revealed.

Each project is shaped through a quiet alignment of space, material, and lived experience.

VeLUS Interiors Services

VeLUS INTERIORS approaches space as more than composition or form.

Each interior becomes a study in presence, shaped by what is chosen, what is removed, and what is allowed to remain.

There is a quiet belief underlying the work: that interiors are lived environments, and what we surround ourselves with defines how we understand space, memory, and self.

Every project is guided by restraint, clarity, and intention — where nothing is excessive, and nothing is without purpose.

We engage from the earliest architectural stages, contributing spatial direction as structure takes form. Working in alignment with architects and builders, we shape interiors that are considered from the blueprint forward, ensuring clarity of proportion, material intent, and spatial rhythm.
Existing spaces are approached with precision rather than sentiment. We reassess structure, flow, and material presence, refining what already exists into a more coherent spatial language. Each renovation is resolved through context, not repetition, allowing the architecture to dictate its own evolution.
We compose complete interior environments through a disciplined layering of furnishings, lighting, and material tone. Each selection is intentional within the broader spatial composition, resulting in interiors that feel resolved, continuous, and quietly intentional in their function and form.
