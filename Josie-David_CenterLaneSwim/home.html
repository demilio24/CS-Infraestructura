WHO WE ARE

Swimming Lessons For Every Stage

At Center Lane Swim School, we believe every child deserves to feel safe and confident in the water. Our curriculum is designed to meet swimmers where they are — from first-time babies to advanced competitive swimmers.

  • All instructors are Red Cross Certified
  • Small groups — max 3 students per instructor
  • Proprietary curriculum from infant to advanced
  • 4 Swim Currents for unique training environment
  • Underwater mirrors for instructor feedback

WHY CENTER LANE

A Swim School Built On Care

Founded by Josie & David Howdyshell, Center Lane Swim School was born from 18+ years of passion for teaching children to be safe and confident in the water.

Josie specializes in working with fearful swimmers, creating an environment where every child feels encouraged and supported. David ensures operations run smoothly across all locations, maintaining the highest standards of safety and excellence.

Together, they've built a team that treats every student like family.

Safety First

Every decision starts with your child's safety. Small groups, certified instructors, and constant supervision.

Kindness & Patience

We meet every child where they are. Fearful swimmers get extra care and time to build confidence.

Encouraging

Positive reinforcement fuels progress. Every milestone is celebrated — big or small.

Fun & Uplifting

Learning to swim should be joyful. Our lessons are designed to keep kids smiling and excited.

Swim Currents Tech

4 adjustable swim currents create a unique training environment that builds endurance faster than traditional pools.

Small Groups (3:1)

Maximum 3 students per instructor ensures personalized attention and faster progress for every swimmer.
