Why Center Lane Swim School?

We believe every child deserves to feel safe and confident in the water. Our curriculum is designed to meet swimmers where they are, from first-time babies to stroke-development swimmers ready to graduate to a swim team.

  • All instructors are Red Cross Certified
  • Small groups: max 3 students per instructor
  • Proprietary curriculum from infant to advanced
  • Unassisted swimming: no floaties or flippers as crutches
  • The Hump Guarantee: free extra lessons if your swimmer is still nervous after 4–5 sessions
  • Adaptive lessons for swimmers with special needs (state grant funds accepted)
  • 4 Swim Currents for unique training environment
  • Underwater mirrors for instructor feedback
